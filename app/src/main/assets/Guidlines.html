• FloatingWidget : 
    Hold to move anywhere.

    Long press to maximize.

    Tap anywhere on screen to minimize.

    Hold and move below center of screen to remove FloatingWidget.
  • PlayingView1 : 
    Swipe up and down to play prev/next.

    Slide right from playingView to view available lyrics.

    Slide right to view queuelist.

    Drag,Drop and remove(from right) queueList.
  • PlayingView2 : 
    Slide right/left from playingView to view available lyrics.
  • PlayingView3 : 
    Slide up/down from albumcover to view/hide available lyrics.

    Drag,Drop and remove(from right) queueList.
  • Equalizer : 
    Slide right/left from bottom to see other effects
